Homerules
During our pathfinder games we generally use the homerules. Be aware that a Gamemaster is free to apply different rules for their games!
  1. The following classes are banned:
    • Chained Summoner
  2. Unchained skills are available for all classes, with the exception of Intimidate.
    To compensate for this, unchained rogues receive Skill Focus as a bonus skill when they would normally gain Rogue's Edge.
