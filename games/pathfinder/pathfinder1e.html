Homerules
During our pathfinder games we generally use these homerules. Be aware that a Gamemaster is free to enforce different rules for their games!
  1. The following classes are banned:
    • Chained Summoner
  2. Unchained skills are available for all classes, with the exception of Intimidate. The rank requirement remains unchanged.
    When unchained rogues would normally gain Rogue's Edge, they instead receive Skill Focus as a bonus feat.
  3. You can open doors as part of a move action.
  4. Paladin and antipaladin can be any good or any evil alignment respectively.
  5. We use modified favored class bonusses (FCB) for the following classes:
    • Ninja: The ninja can take any FCB for the rogue and monk classes. Rogue talents gained from FCB will be ninja talents.
    • Antipaladin: Antipaladins can choose any FCB for the paladin class. Any FCB that affects abilities such as Smite Evil will instead apply to the antipaladin's abilities such as Smite Good.
    • Samurai: The samurai can take any FCB for the cavalier class.
